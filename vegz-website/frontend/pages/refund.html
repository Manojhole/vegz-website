Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: January 2025 | Vegz Private Limited

At Vegz, we stand behind the quality of every delivery. Because we deal in fresh perishable produce, our refund policy is designed to be fair to both buyers and the farmers who supply us.

Our Quality Guarantee

Every batch of vegetables is sorted and graded before dispatch. If what you receive does not match the quality you ordered, we will make it right — immediately. No long processes, no back-and-forth. Just call us.

Refund Scenarios

How to Report an Issue

Quality complaints must be reported within 2 hours of delivery. After this window, we cannot process replacements as the produce may have been stored improperly.

Refund Processing

For UPI payments, refunds are processed back to the original UPI ID within 2–3 business days. For COD orders, refunds are issued as credit against your next order or via UPI transfer if you provide us with your UPI ID.

No-Refund Cases

  • Quality complaints raised after 2 hours of delivery
  • Produce damaged after delivery due to improper storage by the buyer
  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Incorrect quantity ordered by the buyer

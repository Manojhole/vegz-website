Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 | Vegz Private Limited

Vegz Private Limited ("Vegz", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website at vegz.online or our mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our service:

  • Mobile number: Used for OTP-based login and delivery communication
  • Business name and type: Provided optionally in your profile
  • Delivery address: Required to complete your order
  • Order history: Items ordered, amounts, and delivery status
  • Location (optional): If you use the map pin-drop feature, we store your latitude/longitude to improve delivery accuracy
  • Device information: Browser type and IP address for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via OTP login
  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order confirmations and delivery updates via call or WhatsApp
  • To improve our product catalogue and pricing based on order patterns
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on AWS EC2 servers located in India (Mumbai region). Our database server is on a private network — not accessible from the public internet. All API communications are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

4. OTP & Authentication

When you request an OTP, the code is hashed (encrypted) before storage. The raw OTP is never stored in our database — only the secure hash. OTPs expire in 10 minutes and are single-use only.

5. Cookies

We use only essential session storage (not tracking cookies) to maintain your login state. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • MSG91 / Twilio: For sending OTP SMS — your mobile number is shared with the SMS provider only to deliver the OTP
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet.js: For map display — no personal data is shared; map tiles are loaded from OSM servers
  • Google Fonts: For typography — no personal data is shared

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@vegz.online with the subject "Data Request".

8. Data Retention

We retain your account and order data for 3 years for accounting and dispute resolution purposes. OTP session records are automatically purged after 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for business use only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
